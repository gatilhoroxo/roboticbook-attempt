# Foundations
## Identity
**Purpose**
- To build a deep, connected and lasting understanding of Medical Robotics through engineering thinking, scientific curiosity and continuous reflection.
**Philosophy**
- Learn through questions. Connect knowledge instead of collecting information.
**Type**
- Living Knowledge System
**Audience**
- A future Medical Robotics researcher.


## Guiding Philosophy
**The Medical Robotics Journey Handbook exists to transform isolated information into connected understanding.**
*Knowledge* should answer meaningful questions, connect to previous ideas, and build toward real engineering and medical problems.
*Progress* is measured not only by what is known, but by the ability to explain, apply, connect, question, and improve that knowledge.
This *handbook* is a living system that evolves together with its author.
